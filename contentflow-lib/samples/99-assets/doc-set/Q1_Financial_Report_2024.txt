ACME CORPORATION
Q1 2024 QUARTERLY FINANCIAL REPORT
Period: January 1 - March 31, 2024
Prepared by: Finance Department

=============================================================
EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
=============================================================

ACME Corporation delivered a strong start to fiscal year 2024.
Revenue reached $10.5 million, representing a 12% year-over-year
increase driven primarily by enterprise sales growth in the
North American market.

=============================================================
FINANCIAL HIGHLIGHTS
=============================================================

Revenue:              $10,500,000
Cost of Goods Sold:   $4,200,000
Gross Profit:         $6,300,000
Operating Expenses:   $4,000,000
EBITDA:               $3,100,000
Net Income:           $2,300,000
Profit Margin:        21.9%

Headcount:            245 employees
New Hires (Q1):       12
Customer Count:       1,250

=============================================================
REVENUE BREAKDOWN BY SEGMENT
=============================================================

Enterprise Sales:     $6,300,000  (60%)
Mid-Market:           $2,625,000  (25%)
SMB / Self-Serve:     $1,575,000  (15%)

=============================================================
KEY INITIATIVES & MILESTONES
=============================================================

1. EMEA Market Expansion
   - Opened London office in February 2024
   - Hired regional sales director and 3 account executives
   - Pipeline of $2.1M generated in first 2 months

2. Product Launches
   - ContentFlow Pro v3.0 released (Jan 15)
   - Analytics Dashboard v2.0 beta launched (Mar 1)

3. Customer Wins
   - Signed 3 enterprise contracts > $500K ARR
   - Customer retention rate: 94.2%

=============================================================
RISK FACTORS
=============================================================

- Increasing competition in mid-market segment
- EMEA expansion costs expected to impact Q2 margins
- Supply chain delays affecting hardware procurement

=============================================================
OUTLOOK
=============================================================

Management expects continued growth in Q2 with revenue
guidance of $11.0M - $11.5M. EMEA is expected to contribute
10-15% of new revenue by end of Q2.
