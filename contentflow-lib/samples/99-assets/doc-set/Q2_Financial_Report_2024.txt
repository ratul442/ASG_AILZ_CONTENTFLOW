ACME CORPORATION
Q2 2024 QUARTERLY FINANCIAL REPORT
Period: April 1 - June 30, 2024
Prepared by: Finance Department

=============================================================
EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
=============================================================

ACME Corporation maintained strong momentum in Q2, delivering
6.7% quarter-over-quarter revenue growth. Revenue reached
$11.2 million with particularly strong mid-market traction
and early returns from the EMEA expansion initiative.

=============================================================

=============================================================

EBITDA:               $3,500,000

REVENUE BREAKDOWN BY SEGMENT
=============================================================

=============================================================
KEY INITIATIVES & MILESTONES
=============================================================

1. EMEA Market Expansion
   - EMEA contributing 15% of new revenue
   - 42 qualified opportunities in pipeline
   - Expanded to cover DACH region (Germany, Austria, Switzerland)

2. Product Development
   - Analytics Dashboard v2.0 GA release (May 10)
   - Mobile app beta testing with 50 customers
   - API v3 launched with 200% improvement in throughput

3. Customer Growth
   - Net new logos: 90 accounts
   - Enterprise upsells: $1.8M in expansion revenue
   - NPS score improved to 72 (from 68 in Q1)

=============================================================
RISK FACTORS
=============================================================

- Currency headwinds in EMEA (EUR/USD volatility)
- Key competitor launched aggressive pricing in mid-market
- Infrastructure costs rising with customer growth

=============================================================
OUTLOOK
=============================================================

Q3 typically sees seasonal softness due to summer months.
Revenue guidance for Q3: $10.5M - $11.0M. Full-year guidance
raised to $44M - $46M (previously $42M - $44M).
