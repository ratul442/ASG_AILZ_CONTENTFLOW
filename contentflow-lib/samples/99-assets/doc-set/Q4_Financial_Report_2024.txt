ACME CORPORATION
Q4 2024 QUARTERLY FINANCIAL REPORT
Period: October 1 - December 31, 2024
Prepared by: Finance Department

=============================================================
EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
=============================================================

ACME Corporation closed fiscal year 2024 with a record quarter.
Revenue surged 21.3% QoQ to $13.1 million, driven by strong
enterprise deal closures and holiday demand in the SMB segment.
Profit margin recovered to 29.8%, the highest of the year.

KEY INITIATIVES & MILESTONES
=============================================================

1. Enterprise Growth
   - Closed 8 enterprise deals > $200K ARR
   - Largest single deal: $1.2M (3-year contract with Fortune 500)
   - Enterprise deal closures drove 40% of Q4 revenue

2. Product Excellence
   - AI-powered content suggestions GA (Nov 15)
   - Platform processing 2.5M documents/month (up from 1.8M in Q3)
   - Customer-reported bugs down 42% YoY

3. Year-End Metrics
   - Full-year revenue: $45.6M (within guidance range)
   - Full-year net income: $10.8M
   - ARR growth: 28% YoY
   - Customer NPS: 74
